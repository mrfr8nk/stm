## 👥 Four Dedicated Portals

### 🛡️ Administrator Portal
- **Student Management** — Enroll, promote, transfer, and manage all student records
- **Staff Management** — Add teachers, assign classes & subjects
- **Fee Management** — Track payments, generate receipts, manage scholarships
- **Academic Configuration** — Set up classes, subjects, grading scales, academic sessions
- **Reports & Analytics** — School-wide performance dashboards and exportable reports
- **Announcements** — Broadcast messages to the entire school or specific groups
- **Security** — Role-based access control, activity logs, account banning
- **Petty Cash** — Track school expenditures with receipt uploads

### 📚 Teacher Portal
- **Grade Entry** — Enter marks with automatic grade letter calculation
- **Attendance Tracking** — Mark daily attendance per class
- **Report Generation** — Generate professional report cards with QR verification
- **Class Teacher Powers** — Full access to all subjects in assigned class
- **Monthly Tests** — Track continuous assessment throughout the term
- **Record Books** — Custom digital record books for any tracking need
- **Rankings** — View class and subject rankings instantly
- **Messaging** — Direct communication with parents and other teachers

### 🎓 Student Portal
- **View Grades** — See all marks, grades, and teacher comments
- **Attendance Record** — Track personal attendance history
- **Report Cards** — Download official reports anytime
- **Fee Status** — Check fee balances and payment history
- **Rankings** — See class position and performance trends
- **Study Pal** — AI-powered study assistant for homework help
- **Announcements** — Stay updated with school news

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Portal
- **Multi-Child Support** — Link and monitor multiple children
- **Grade Monitoring** — Real-time access to your child's academic performance
- **Attendance Alerts** — Know when your child misses school
- **Fee Tracking** — View balances and payment history
- **Direct Messaging** — Communicate with teachers and administration
- **Report Cards** — Download your child's reports anytime
